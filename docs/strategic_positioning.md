# Strategic Positioning

This project is not trying to beat live commercial flexibility platforms at their own game in Release 1.

Companies such as Octopus/Kraken have live customers, connected assets, telemetry, tariffs, dispatch infrastructure, trading access, operational feedback loops and market participation processes. This repository is currently a transparent modelling and analysis product, not a live asset-control platform.

## Durable Edge

The project should preserve its edge through:

- transparency;
- explainability;
- open/public-data methodology;
- transparent GB BESS revenue-stack modelling for investors, analysts and public-data due diligence;
- clean audit trail from result to source, assumption and config;
- fast experimentation on scenarios and modelling boundaries;
- investor and analyst tooling rather than live retail flexibility control.

## Product Implication

When product choices conflict, prefer the path that strengthens auditability, explainability and public-data defensibility. Do not add opaque optimisation, black-box forecasts or live-control claims unless the project has the data, controls and verification needed to support them.

The project can later grow into a dashboard or decision-support product, but its core identity remains a transparent GB BESS revenue-stack modelling engine.
